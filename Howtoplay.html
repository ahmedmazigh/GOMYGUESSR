How to play

Start by selecting your preferred difficulty level: easy with 8 cards, medium with 16 cards, or hard with 20 cards. Once you've made your choice,Flip the cards one by one and use your memory skills to uncover matching pairs. Remember where each card is located to make successful matches. The goal is to clear the board by finding all the pairs within the shortest time possible. It's a test of concentration and memory prowess. Are you up for the challenge? Let the card flipping begin!

Scoring System & Leaderboard

Get ready to compete and climb to the top! Gomyguessr features an exciting scoring system and leaderboard that adds an extra layer of challenge and fun.As you strive to match pairs, keep an eye on the clock and the number of moves you make. The faster you complete the game and the fewer moves you use, the higher your score will be. Test your skills against players from around the world and see your name rise on the global leaderboard. Can you secure the top spot?
